Instructions...

  • Choose a player to go first.
  • The first player marks an empty cell on the 3x3 grid with their symbol (X or O).
  • The second player then marks an empty cell with their symbol.
  • Players take turns marking empty cells until one player achieves a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of three of their symbols, or until all cells are filled.
  • If a player achieves a winning line, they are declared the winner.
  • If all cells are filled without a winner, the game is a draw.
  • Players can strategize to block their opponent while aiming to create their own winning line.
  • The game ends when a player achieves a winning line or when all cells are filled.
